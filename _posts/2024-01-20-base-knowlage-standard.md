## Digital Transformation

| Concept        | Description                                                                                                     |
| -------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Digital Transformation | Use of digital technologies to change business models and provide new revenue and value-producing opportunities |
| Industry 4.0   | Fourth industrial revolution characterized by automation and data exchange                                          |
| IIoT           | Industrial Internet of Things, network of industrial devices with software and sensors                              |
| Smart Factory  | Digitalized production facility with autonomous systems and real-time data exchange                                   |
| Digital Twin   | Virtual representation of physical asset, process, or system                                                    |
| Big Data       | Extremely large datasets that may be analyzed computationally to reveal patterns, trends, and associations      |
| AI/ML          | Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning, systems that can learn from data and make decisions                      |
